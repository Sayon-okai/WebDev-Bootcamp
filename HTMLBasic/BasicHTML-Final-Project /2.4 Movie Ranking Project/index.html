My top 3 movies of all-time

Superman

Superman is a 1978 superhero film directed by Richard Donner, based on the DC Comics character of the same name. It stars Christopher Reeve as Superman and Margot Kidder as Lois Lane.

Batman

Batman is a 1989 superhero film directed by Tim Burton, based on the DC Comics character of the same name. It stars Michael Keaton as Batman and Jack Nicholson as the Joker.

Red Notice

Red Notice is a 2021 action-comedy film directed by Rawson Marshall Thurber. It stars Dwayne Johnson, Ryan Reynolds, and Gal Gadot in a story about an Interpol agent tracking down the world's most wanted art thief.
